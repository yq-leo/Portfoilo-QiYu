Static Job-shop Scheduling for Industry

Formulated the static Job-Shop Scheduling Problem (JSP) into a programmable model in Factor Graph; Proposed a derivative of distributed max-sum algorithm to iteratively solve the programmable model. Proposed algorithm capable of resolving large-scale JSPs in an industrial environment with a 3-time faster speed.

Tech: C++, Concurrent Programming, Job-shop Scheduling
